A Music Quiz Game

    About Track Master

    Track Master is an online music trivia game designed for fans who love testing their music knowledge. There are millions of artists available to choose from; As long as they are on spotify, you can play their discography in Track Master.

    The project was built as a fun way to blend music and technology. It combines web development, APIs, and a custom leaderboard system so that players can compete with friends and music lovers around the world.

    How to Play

    1. Click the "Play" link to start playing
    2. Use the search bar on the Home page to find your favorite artist.
    3. Select an album to start a round of trivia. Or select their full discography for an extra challenge.
    4. You’ll be presented with a list of songs. Try to guess the correct track titles as quickly as possible.
    5. Earn points for each correct answer — faster guesses score higher!
    6. If you can't name every song, hit the "Give up" button and try again!
    7. If you correctly guess every song, add your name to the leaderboard to see how you compare with other players.

    Track Master is free to play and updates regularly with new music content. Whether you’re into pop, rock, hip hop, or indie, there’s always something new to discover.
